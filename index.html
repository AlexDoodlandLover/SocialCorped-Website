Welcome to SocialCorped!

SocialCorped is your all-in-one platform for connecting with others, sharing your life, enjoying fun games, and exploring a world of interests. Whether you want to chat with friends, find new communities, or simply unwind with a quick game, SocialCorped brings it all together in one intuitive app.

Key Features

Lexi Chat

  • Direct Messaging: Engage in private conversations with individual users.
  • Rich Communication: Share screenshots and other media directly within chats.
  • User Profiles: See who you're chatting with through their profile pictures.

LexiVideos & Content Sharing

  • Video Uploads: Easily share your videos with the community.
  • Topic-Based Discussions: Create and participate in discussions on various topics.

Server Finder & Chat Categories

  • Diverse Communities: Explore and join chat rooms based on a wide array of categories such as Business, Computers & Web, Education, Film & TV, Games, Health, Home, Leisure, Music & Radio, Science & Technology, Society, Sports, and Travel.
  • Popular Chats: Discover trending chat rooms and connect with others who share your interests.

Engaging Games

  • Hangman: Test your vocabulary with classic Hangman, featuring different categories. Earn diamonds and trophies as you play!
  • Catch The Snacks: A fun, fast-paced game where you catch falling snacks. Aim for a high score!

The Forums

  • Structured Discussions: Participate in organized forums for Q&A, Venting, App Help, Text Based RPGs, Show And Tell, and Flex Things.
  • Community Support: Get help, share ideas, and connect with other users in dedicated topic areas.
